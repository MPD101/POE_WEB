The History and When It Was Established

The business was established in 2023 and has grown rapidly over the past year. Starting small from home in the city of Port Elizabeth, the business has since worked on and tuned multiple cars from major companies like Volkswagen, Toyota, and BMW. To this day, these large companies send their newest car models for tuning and full inspection to ensure they are ready for the new owners.

The Goal

The goal of the business is to be profitable and to have at least over 500 customers while selling our parts worldwide by partnering with more profitable companies that span across countries. Another goal is to receive positive feedback from our customers and to expand the workshop to a better location, employ more workers, and strengthen their skills in the automotive industry.

The Vision

The vision of the business is to be the most customer-centric automotive brand around the city, where every vehicle created is designed with our customers' needs and aspirations in mind, ensuring safety, reliability, comfort, and enjoyment on every journey.

The Mission

The mission of the business is to supply top-quality automotive parts and accessories with a commitment to innovation, excellence, and customer satisfaction. We aim to enable our partners and customers to achieve optimal performance and safety, while also delivering reliable, efficient, and honest automotive repair and maintenance services, ensuring every vehicle we service operates at its best.
